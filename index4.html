About AHSIS

Bridging Innovation, Sustainability & Community

Founded in 2025, AHSIS is a DPIIT-recognized startup dedicated to bridging the gap between innovation, sustainability, and community development.

We harness the power of geospatial intelligence, Earth observation technologies, and data-driven innovation to address complex challenges in natural resource management, climate resilience, Agrotech, and rural development.
